Friday Saturday

friday

9:00am -> 5:30pm

Remembering SCRC14

Let's all (really all) talk about what this Ruby conference is for, why you're here, and what you're going to do to go home thrilled with your experience. First, listen to my perspective - I say this conference is for strengthening and broadening our sense of ourselves as Rubyists so that we can learn from each other and move forward as a community. Then wield your markers and stickies to start to express and share your ideas about what will make SCRC14 amazing for you and for us

10:00am -> 4:30pm

Steel City Ruby Open Development

Are you Stuck on a hard problem, interested in Discussions about development, or want to pick the brain of a fellow developer? Steel City Ruby has set up an area for open development. Giving anyone interested in a collaborative space to share ideas and get answers!

ModCloth Dual Track Party

Thanks to ModCloth we are going to have a dual track path this year!! Come by PNC Park, the home of the Pittsburgh Pirates, after the conference. If you are into a little friendly competition bring your favorite board.There will be second room setup where you can challenge your fellow developers to a ??friendly?? game of what-have-you. In another room, there will be stimulating conversation, witty jabs, and jokes of all sorts. Friends, co-workers, and new acquaintances alike can partake in dining and drinks, sharing an amazing view of the Pittsburgh skyline and Ball Park.

Jam Session in honor of Jim Weirich

Bring your instruments! In honor of our late friend Jim Weirich, we will be dedicating the stage to a jam session. Jim was known for bringing his ukelele to conferences and hosting impromptu one. So we're going to have a spot in our schedule for others to continue this trend.
